The City That Never Sleep

Night City never sleeps, glowing with dazzling neon lights and adorned with captivating animated images on its buildings. Modern technological advancements have made holograms a part of everyday life, while advanced systems control the city's perpetual rainy and overcast weather. Suspended trains glide smoothly across the sky, connecting the city's bustling streets with its shadowy districts. Realistic electronic stores are found on every corner, while the abundance of dark alleys gives the city a mysterious vibe, offering a unique experience where the line between reality and the virtual world blurs.
